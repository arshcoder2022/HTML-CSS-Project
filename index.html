Discover Majestic India

One of the oldest civilizations in the world, India is a mosaic of multicultural experiences. With a rich heritage and myriad attractions, the country is among the most popular tourist destinations in the world. As the 7th largest country in the world, India stands apart from the rest of Asia, marked off as it is by mountains and the sea, which give the country a distinct geographical entity.

This website will give you all the information you need to plan your visit to India:

  • A brief summary of the area, with highlights of important places.
  • Guide on local food and cuisines.
  • Information about the local culture and traditions.
  • Hotel recommendations with web links to hotel and booking sites.
