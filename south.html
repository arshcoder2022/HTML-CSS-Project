South India

South India, also known as Dakshina Bharata or Peninsular India, consists of the southern part of India encompassing the Indian states of Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Telangana, as well as the union territories of Lakshadweep and Puducherry. Like a giant wedge plunging into the ocean, South India is the subcontinent's steamy heartland – a lush contrast to the peaks and plains up north. South India is a mystic and magnificent destination that combines bright colors, fascinating cultures, and beautiful landscapes, all largely undiscovered by tourists. Mysore Palace is one of the most visited sites in South India. Let's explore!

Mysore Palace

Mysore is commonly described as the "City of Palaces.”

An incredibly breathtaking example of the Indo - Saracenic style of architecture, the Mysore Palace is a magnificent edifice located in Mysore in the state of Karnataka. Also known as the Amba Vilas Palace, it is the former palace of the royal family of Mysore and is still their official residence. Mysore Palace was built in 1912 for the 24th Ruler of the Wodeyar Dynasty and is counted as one of the biggest palaces in the country.

The facade of the palace is a harmonious blend of Hindu, Muslim, Rajput and Gothic styles, which imparts it a regal quality. With the Chamundi Hills towards its eastern side, the spectacle of the Mysore Palace is an enchanting sight to behold. Needless to say, it is the second most visited historical monument visited by local and foreign tourists after the Taj Mahal. Presently located inside the Old Fort, Mysore Palace is renowned for its light & sound show and vibrant Dussehra celebrations.

Best time to visit

October to February is one of the best times to visit Mysore. These months have pleasant weather as a respite from the intense heat of summer. With these months captivating the city's beauty, summers are less ideal for traveling and sightseeing. December is considered the coldest month, with the temperature dipping in the evenings.

Visiting Hours

Mysore Palace Visiting Hours :

Mysore Palace is open for visitors everyday between 10:00 a.m to 5:30 p.m . The best time to visit the place is in night on Sundays and public holidays, from 7 PM to 7.45 PM, when the palace is illuminated.

Food and Cuisine

South Indian Breakfast:

South Indian cuisine is known for its vibrant colors, fantastic flavor and tastes ranging from sweet to sour to spicy. They are majorly centered on rice or rice-based dishes. Some examples of popular South Indian dishes include Idli, Dosa, Vada, Appam, Rasam, Uttapam, etc. Since South Indian dishes are mainly fermented - which is healthy for gut microbiota - they are considered a perfect breakfast option. The fermentation process improves digestibility, enhances B vitamins and boosts the immune system.


Mysore Pak :

Mysore Pak is an Indian sweet prepared in ghee. It originated in the city of Mysore. It is made of generous amounts of ghee, sugar, gram flour, and often cardamom. The texture of this sweet is similar to a buttery and dense cookie. Mysore pak is traditionally made during weddings.
